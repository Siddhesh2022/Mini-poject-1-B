About 12th Science

12th Science is a high school course that covers a range of scientific disciplines including biology, chemistry, physics, and mathematics. Students who take this course are provided with a solid foundation in these fields and are prepared for further study or careers in science-related fields.

Courses

  • Biology

    Biology is the study of living organisms and their interactions with each other and the environment. Topics covered include genetics, evolution, ecology, and human biology.

  • Chemistry

    Chemistry is the study of matter and its properties, composition, and reactions. Topics covered include atomic structure, chemical bonding, reactions, and organic chemistry.

  • Physics

    Physics is the study of matter, energy, and their interactions. Topics covered include mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and quantum mechanics.

  • Mathematics

    Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, and shapes. Topics covered include algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and calculus.
