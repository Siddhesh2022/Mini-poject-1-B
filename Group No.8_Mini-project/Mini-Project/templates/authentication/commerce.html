About 12th Commerce

12th Commerce is an academic program that focuses on business and economics-related subjects. It is usually a two-year program that is taken after completing 10th grade. Some of the subjects that are typically taught in 12th Commerce include:

  • Accountancy
  • Economics
  • Business Studies
  • Mathematics
  • Computer Science

Upon completion of the program, students may pursue further education in fields such as business administration, accounting, finance, or economics, or they may choose to enter the workforce in roles such as sales, marketing, or finance.

Career Options After 12th Commerce

Upon completing 12th Commerce, students can pursue a variety of career paths, including:
