About 12th Arts

12th Arts is an academic program that focuses on humanities and social science-related subjects. It is usually a two-year program that is taken after completing 10th grade. Some of the subjects that are typically taught in 12th Arts include:

  • History
  • Geography
  • Sociology
  • Political Science
  • Psychology

Upon completion of the program, students may pursue further education in fields such as arts, humanities, social sciences, education, or law, or they may choose to enter the workforce in roles such as journalism, writing, public relations, or social work.
